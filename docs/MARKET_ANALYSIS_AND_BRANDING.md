## 4. Pricing Strategy Analysis

### Market Pricing Tiers

**Budget Tier** ($7-45/month)
- Moosend: $7/mo
- HubSpot: $45/mo
- Target: Solo creators, small businesses
- Features: Basic AI, limited usage

**Mid-Market** ($50-150/month)
- Jasper: $39/mo (starter), $99/mo (pro)
- Surfer SEO: $19-249/mo
- CoSchedule: $29/user/mo
- Target: Marketing teams, agencies
- Features: Advanced AI, team collaboration

**Enterprise** ($200+/month)
- ActiveCampaign: $145/mo
- Keap: $249/mo
- Sprout Social: $199/seat/mo
- Target: Large teams, enterprises
- Features: Custom workflows, white label, API

### Our Pricing Model Options

**Option 1: Usage-Based (Recommended)**
- **Rationale**: Transparent, fair, scales with value
- **Structure**:
  - Free: 5 topics/month (trial)
  - Starter: $29/mo - 20 topics (~$1.45/topic)
  - Pro: $79/mo - 100 topics (~$0.79/topic)
  - Agency: $199/mo - 500 topics (~$0.40/topic)
- **Add-ons**: Extra topics $0.50 each, image generation +$0.05/image
- **Pros**: Fair, predictable, competitive with Jasper
- **Cons**: Requires usage tracking

**Option 2: Flat Subscription**
- **Structure**:
  - Solo: $49/mo - Unlimited topics, 1 user, 3 brands
  - Team: $99/mo - Unlimited, 5 users, 10 brands
  - Agency: $249/mo - Unlimited, 15 users, unlimited brands
- **Pros**: Simpler, predictable revenue
- **Cons**: Abuse potential (unlimited usage)

**Option 3: Hybrid (Best of Both)**
- **Structure**:
  - Starter: $39/mo - 50 topics, 1 user, 3 brands
  - Pro: $99/mo - 200 topics, 5 users, 10 brands
  - Agency: $249/mo - 1000 topics, unlimited users, unlimited brands
- **Add-ons**: Extra topics $0.30/each
- **Pros**: Predictable + fair overages
- **Cons**: Slightly complex

### Recommended: **Option 3 (Hybrid)**
- Competitive with Jasper ($39 entry)
- Fair overages (cheaper than competitors)
- Room for enterprise custom pricing
